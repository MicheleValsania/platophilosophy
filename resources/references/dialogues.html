Plato's Dialogues

This is a complete guide to all of Plato's dialogues, organized by period. Each dialogue links to free translations available online in English (MIT Classics) and Italian (LiberLiber).

Dialogues marked with a star (‚òÖ) are recommended starting points for beginners.

New to Plato? Start with the Apology, then Crito, then Meno. These three dialogues (70 minutes total) will introduce you to Socratic questioning, philosophical method, and why reading primary texts matters.
