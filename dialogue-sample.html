What this dialogue aims to do

Not to lecture. Not to “win” an argument. But to help you clarify what you mean — and see where it leads.

How the guidance changes

  • Beginner: gentle prompts, simple distinctions, clear summaries.
  • Intermediate: definitions tested with examples and counterexamples.
